# Landing Page Hot Tips #26-50

## #26 - Use Fewer Fonts
Multiple typefaces with multiple weights = unnecessary load time.

**Action:** Pair your primary typeface with a native system font.

Fast-loading + organized typeset = classy and considerate.

**Resources:**
- System Font Stacks - code snippets by Geoff Graham
- Typewolf - typography inspiration
- 40 Best Google Fonts - curated with previews

---

## #27 - Step Into Your Visitor's Shoes
**Checklist:**
- [ ] Load landing page on mobile
- [ ] Read the text aloud
- [ ] Use the navigation
- [ ] Complete checkout successfully

This exposes friction points. Once confident, do #18 (watch real user).

---

## #28 - Space Using Ratios
Set a base size (e.g., 8px) and define padding as multiples:

- Tiny: 8px
- Small: 16px
- Medium: 32px
- Large: 64px

Using ratios aligns content better and tightens design.

**Resource:** 8-Point Grid System - visual breakdown by Elliot Dahl

---

## #29 - Add Personality
People want to support people.

**Ideas:**
- Portrait of person who built the service
- Photo of desk where posters were illustrated
- Animated signature
- Behind-the-scenes glimpses

**Resources:**
- Fiverr - get signature animation for $5
- VideoTouch - corner video explaining product (free up to 5k visits)

---

## #30 - Curate Your FAQs
Unsorted FAQs don't help confused visitors.

**Rules:**
1. Frequently asked ONLY
2. Ordered by popularity
3. Pre-sale questions first (move post-sale to support page)

---

## #31 - Replace GIFs with Video
Video offers:
- Better performance
- More colors
- Smaller file size
- Ability to pause

"Easier to produce" is not a reason to use GIFs on landing pages.

**Resources:**
- ImageOptim - GIF optimization if necessary
- Screenflow - screen recording, exports to lite MP4

---

## #32 - Offer an Annual Discount
12-month commitment = cash flow boost for SaaS.

**Implementation:**
- Monthly/Annual toggle switcher
- Clear "Save 10%" badge on annual tab
- Test thoroughly on mobile

---

## #33 - Deconstruct Your About Paragraph
**Before:**
"Citrus Studio creates multi-faceted online business software using modern, cutting-edge technologies to help businesses stay on track and achieve their dreams."

**Step 1 - Strip to core:**
"Citrus Studio builds goal-tracking software."

**Step 2 - Add one impactful adjective:**
"Citrus Studio builds effective goal-tracking software."

## #34 - Run a Regular Speed Test
Speed tests highlight excessive fonts, images, or scripts.

**Tools:**
- Google PageSpeed Insights
- Pingdom Speed Test

Poor results = to-do list. Make it a game.

---

## #35 - Put Your Name on It
Common visitor concerns:
- "If I commit, will I get help?"
- "Who actually built this?"
- "Are they continuously improving?"

Taking ownership eases fears and shows confidence.

**Resource:** "Sign your work" - Seth Godin blog post

---

## #36 - Align with a Grid
Layout grids keep landing pages tidy by positioning within constraints.

**Resources:**
- Material Design - columns, gutters, margins explained
- 8-Point Grid System - benefits breakdown
- CSS Grid Layout Guide - CSS-Tricks comprehensive guide
- History of grids in Graphic Design - Wikipedia

---

## #37 - Add a Splash of Color
A color burst commands attention and adds excitement.

When excited, people try new things.

**Resources:**
- BGJar - free tool for colorful SVG blobs
- Color Scheme Inspiration - 700+ examples

---

## #38 - Invest in an Icon Library
Go-to icon pack = faster development + consistent aesthetic.

**Resources:**
- Iconfinder - find style, then purchase
- Streamline Icons - 30,000+ icons, 3 weights
- Font Awesome - quality library, impressive UI

---

## #39 - Place Copy Before Images on Mobile
On mobile, visitors see what loads first.

Your value proposition should appear before decorative imagery.

**Action:** Review mobile layout - ensure key copy is above the fold.

---

## #40 - Offer a Free Teaser
Reduce risk with sample content:
- Free chapter
- Demo video
- Trial period
- Sample templates

Let quality speak for itself.

---

## #41 - Code Your Own Social Share Buttons
Third-party social buttons:
- Add tracking scripts
- Slow page load
- Often look inconsistent

Custom buttons = faster, cleaner, on-brand.

---

## #42 - Add Open Graph Meta Tags
Control how your page appears when shared on social media.

**Essential tags:**
```html
<meta property="og:title" content="Your Title">
<meta property="og:description" content="Your description">
<meta property="og:image" content="https://yoursite.com/image.jpg">
<meta property="og:url" content="https://yoursite.com/page">
```

---

## #43 - Emphasize Time-Saving
Time is valuable. If your product saves time, quantify it.

- "Save 4 hours per week"
- "Set up in 5 minutes"
- "Automate what takes you 2 hours daily"

---

## #44 - Tighten Your Big Type
Large headlines often have loose letter-spacing by default.

**Action:** Reduce letter-spacing on headlines for a tighter, more professional look.

Typical adjustment: -0.02em to -0.05em

---

## #45 - Don't Neglect Retina Optimization
Blurry images on high-DPI screens scream unprofessional.

**Action:** Export images at 2x resolution, display at 1x size.

---

## #46 - Remove the Obstacles to Demo
Every step between visitor and demo is a leak.

**Audit:**
- Can they try without signing up?
- Is credit card required for trial?
- How many fields in signup form?

Reduce friction ruthlessly.

---

## #47 - Lower the Risk to Commit
Address commitment fears directly:
- Money-back guarantee
- Free trial period
- Cancel anytime
- No credit card required

Display these prominently near CTA.

---

## #48 - Keep Brand Capitalization Consistent
Pick a style and stick with it:
- "Landing Page Hot Tips"
- "Landing page hot tips"
- "LANDING PAGE HOT TIPS"

Inconsistency looks careless.

---

## #49 - Keep Vertical Spacing Even
Uneven spacing between sections feels chaotic.

**Action:** Use consistent spacing values throughout (see #28 ratios).

---

## #50 - Suggest a Pricing Option
Too many equal options = decision paralysis.

**Action:**
- Highlight recommended option
- Use "Most Popular" badge
- Make one tier visually prominent

Guide the choice.
