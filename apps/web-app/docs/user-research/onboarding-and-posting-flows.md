# Onboarding & Posting Flows

> Detailed flows for team lead onboarding, company onboarding, and the team/opportunity posting lifecycle

---

## Core Principles

### 1. Onboarding Must Be Complete
- Team leads must create a team profile as part of onboarding (not optional)
- Company users must create a company profile as part of onboarding (not optional)
- Incomplete onboarding = limited platform access

### 2. Teams Must Be "Ready" Before Posting
- A team cannot be posted/published until ALL members have:
  - Accepted their invitation
  - Created their individual profiles
  - Confirmed their team membership
- This ensures companies only see complete, committed teams

### 3. Encourage Action, Don't Force It
- Encourage team leads to invite members immediately (but don't block)
- Encourage companies to post opportunities (but don't require)
- Use progress indicators and prompts to drive completion
