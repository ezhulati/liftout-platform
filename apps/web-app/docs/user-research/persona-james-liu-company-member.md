## 7. James's Unique Perspective

### What He Sees That Sarah Doesn't

James spends more time in the weeds with candidates. He notices:

**Pattern 1: Response time correlation**
> "Teams that respond within 24 hours almost always move forward. The ones that take 5 days usually ghost."

**Pattern 2: Team lead vs. team dynamics**
> "Sometimes the team lead is super responsive but when you meet the team, there's tension. I've started paying attention to how team members interact, not just the lead."

**Pattern 3: Salary anchoring**
> "When teams open with their max salary expectation, there's no room to negotiate. The ones that give ranges tend to be more flexible on other terms too."

### His Hiring Philosophy

> "In traditional recruiting, I'm evaluating individuals. Here, I'm evaluating chemistry. It's a different skill. You're watching for micro-expressions in group interviews - who defers to whom, who interrupts, who gets talked over."
